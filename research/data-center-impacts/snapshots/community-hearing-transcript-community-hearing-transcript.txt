GREENFIELD COUNTY PLANNING COMMISSION (fixture local government)
Public Hearing Transcript — Proposed Ridgeway Data Center Campus
Date: 2026-03-05. Case PC-26-018. Excerpts.

COMMISSIONER OKAFOR: The applicant's acoustic study shows 55 dBA at the nearest
property line during full cooling load, which exceeds our 50 dBA nighttime
residential limit without the requested variance.

MS. TRAN (resident, Maple Hollow Road): The generator testing last month at the
existing facility ran for six hours. Our windows rattled. We were told testing
would be monthly; the permit log shows nine test events in the last quarter.

MR. DELGADO (county engineer): Construction traffic is projected at 340 truck
trips per day for 22 months on Route 9, which is currently rated for 190.

MS. WHITFIELD (housing coalition): Since the first campus was announced, median
rent within five miles rose 18 percent in two years, while the county average
rose 9 percent. Two mobile-home parks received redevelopment notices.

COMMISSIONER OKAFOR: The applicant projects 62 permanent operations jobs and
approximately 1,100 construction jobs at peak. County staff verified the
construction figure against contractor filings but could not verify the
permanent figure, which relies on the applicant's staffing model.
