2026 General Rate Case — Filing Summary

Publisher: Interior Plains Power Cooperative (fixture utility). Filed: 2026-04-02. Docket UR-26-233.

The cooperative requests recovery of 480 million dollars in transmission upgrades, of which 62 percent is driven by three contracted data-center campuses.

Under the proposed allocation, residential bills rise 3.1 percent in 2027; without a new large-load rate class the residential increase would be 7.8 percent.

The filing creates a Large Flexible Load rider requiring data-center customers to commit to 12-year minimum contracts with take-or-pay provisions covering 85 percent of contracted capacity.

Intervenor testimony from the Ratepayer Advocate asserts that the proposed allocation still shifts 41 million dollars of upgrade costs to non-data-center customers over the recovery period.
