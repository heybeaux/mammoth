Air Quality Permit 26-AQ-118 — Ridgeway Campus Backup Generation

Publisher: State Air Quality Bureau (fixture state regulator). Issued: 2026-02-19.

The permit authorizes 44 diesel-fired emergency generators totaling 132 MW of standby capacity.

Combined potential to emit is capped at 94 tonnes of nitrogen oxides per year, with readiness testing limited to 100 hours per generator per year and prohibited on forecast high-ozone days.

Modeled one-hour nitrogen dioxide concentrations at the nearest residence reach 82 percent of the health-based standard under simultaneous-testing conditions, which the permit therefore prohibits; no more than six generators may test concurrently.

The bureau received comments requesting battery or gas alternatives; the response document states that the applicant committed to evaluate battery storage for 25 percent of standby capacity at the next permit renewal.
