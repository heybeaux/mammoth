# Mammoth Autonomous Delivery Loop

## Purpose

This loop turns the approved core thesis into working product outcomes. It replaces
phase-shaped delivery with a persistent sequence of product predicates, evidence,
implementation, attack, independent review, and honest reconciliation.

The active outcome is one normal-path, domain-agnostic research run that answers the
world-model holdout question in `AGENTS.md`. The loop continues autonomously until the
completion conditions are proved or a protected authority boundary makes further
progress impossible.

## Durable control plane

Run this work as one managed TaskFlow owned by the requesting Scout session. Persist:

- current product predicate and integration head;
- coordinator session key and run ID;
- active lane session keys, run IDs, worktrees, branches, and path ownership;
- merged commits, PRs, exact-head CI, fresh-main CI, and clean-checkout evidence;
- effect authorities, ceilings, reservations, settlements, and remaining budgets;
- failures, strategy changes, quarantined paths, dissent, and residual risks;
- delivery artifacts and the next automatic continuation step.

A detached worker completing one slice does not complete the flow. The coordinator
must either claim the next predicate, set a truthful protected-boundary wait, or finish
the whole outcome. Never describe a completed one-shot coordinator as an active team.

## Outcome ledger

Maintain `docs/reviews/arbitrary-query-coordinator-ledger.md` as the human-readable
ledger. Each entry records:

1. current product predicate;
2. observable baseline and failing black-box behavior;
3. lane ownership and dependency graph;
4. implementation and review evidence;
5. exact commands and results;
6. integration and CI state;
7. remaining gaps and next claimed predicate.

The ledger reports facts, not intentions. A green test, PR, or exhibition is evidence
for only the predicate it actually exercises.

## Product predicate sequence

```text
1. accepted preview authority binds to an immutable executable plan
2. plan drives discovery and acquisition without topic branches
3. retrieved source content becomes exact spans and independently admitted claims
4. contradictions, gaps, and stop decisions remain plan-relative and inspectable
5. independent agents map mechanisms and cross-domain transfer candidates
6. hypotheses and bounded experiment proposals derive from admitted evidence
7. reader synthesis answers the question with sentence-local citations
8. audit projection preserves complete lineage, residue, cost, and authority
9. unrelated holdouts reject leakage, canned prose, and privileged fixture paths
10. the exact world-model question passes normal-path live review
11. clean-checkout regression, exact-head CI, merge, and fresh-main CI pass
12. receipt records the bounded claim; release waits for a non-Friday day
```

Implement the smallest existing-capability composition that proves the next predicate.
Do not introduce a subsystem until a stocktake shows that current ports cannot support
the required path.

## Execution cycle

1. **Orient** — fetch `origin/main`; inspect thesis, roadmap, audit, ledger, open PRs,
   active workers, authorities, provider readiness, and the highest unproved predicate.
2. **Claim** — record one bounded slice, paths, contracts, dependencies, risks,
   effect ceiling, exact gates, and independent reviewer.
3. **Prepare** — create a fresh worktree from verified main, install with the frozen
   lockfile, and prove model/runtime identity before calling a worker active.
4. **Diverge** — when design or research judgment matters, obtain independent proposals
   before contributors see one another's conclusions.
5. **Build** — implement the smallest contract-to-reader-and-audit vertical slice.
6. **Attack** — test topic leakage, evidence swaps, missing citations, canned prose,
   prompt injection, SSRF/redirect/rebinding, parser hostility, budget races, retries,
   cancellation, crash recovery, tampering, and future schemas as applicable.
7. **Review** — use a worker that did not author the slice. Review product usefulness,
   evidence entailment, authority, hidden topic logic, direct effects, and verifier
   gaming separately.
8. **Verify** — run focused gates, unrelated-domain acceptance, P8/P9 regressions,
   full repository gates, and clean-checkout verification.
9. **Integrate** — review and serially incorporate accepted work; resync dependents;
   push, open/update PR, repair CI, resolve findings, and merge only exact-head green.
10. **Reconcile** — verify remote PR state after merge commands, verify fresh-main CI,
    update TaskFlow and ledger, and record the next unproved predicate.
11. **Continue** — immediately claim the next unblocked slice. Do not wait for the
    human between routine reversible steps.

## Parallelism rules

The coordinator retains the integration slot and uses at most three concurrent lanes.
Parallelize only path-disjoint work whose inputs are already frozen. A useful rhythm is:

- one implementation lane on the next critical product seam;
- one independent adversarial or epistemic review lane;
- one fixture/acceptance or reader-quality lane that can progress without conflicting
  writes.

Do not duplicate unresolved work. Do not give two workers the same worktree or path.
Serialize shared contract, root manifest, and lockfile changes through the coordinator.

## Research-product loop

```text
frame the question and decision
  -> generate independent team and typed plan
  -> validate policy, authority, source classes, and worst-case budget
  -> accept immutable revision
  -> reserve before every governed effect
  -> discover candidates as non-evidence hints
  -> acquire and preserve observed source material
  -> derive bounded spans locally
  -> propose claims by immutable span identity
  -> independently evaluate entailment, lineage, freshness, and contradiction
  -> admit or reject deterministically and preserve residue
  -> map mechanisms across fields with explicit non-equivalences
  -> generate and challenge falsifiable hypotheses
  -> design bounded experiments tied to decisive uncertainties
  -> synthesize admitted-only reader output
  -> compile separate audit, failure, retention, authority, and cost receipts
```

Search results and model prose are never evidence by themselves. Models select among
locally derived bounded spans; they do not author quotes, offsets, evidence IDs, or
lineage. Reports receive only admitted evidence relevant to their governed section.

## Failure and recovery

- Retry transient effects only with stable identity, bounded attempts, and remaining
  reservation. A retry or fallback never creates authority.
- Preserve failed attempts, rejected plans and claims, contradictions, partial output,
  source bytes, diagnostics, uncertain dispatch, and unknown cost.
- After two identical failures, change strategy or request independent diagnosis.
- After three, quarantine the path, document root cause, and schedule another
  independent unblocked predicate rather than weakening a gate.
- Before transferring ownership, verify the exact old run is terminal in the correct
  registry and the worktree is quiescent; terminate a proven orphan writer.
- A timed-out or completed worker can leave useful artifacts. Audit and preserve them
  before replacement.

## Independent acceptance

Before claiming the world-model answer complete, an independent reviewer must read the
report and inspect the audit projection. Mechanical gates must also prove:

- the exact question entered through the normal CLI, not a dedicated exhibition;
- at least three unrelated holdouts use the same generic composition;
- questions produce distinct plans, teams, source choices, claims, and reports;
- hidden canaries, topic phrases, expected answers, and privileged URLs do not leak;
- source removal, mutation, or contradiction changes coverage and conclusions;
- unsupported prose, unresolved citations, huge raw spans, empty answer sections, and
  internal-ID-first reports fail;
- experiment proposals identify the uncertainty, intervention, evaluator, threshold,
  budget, safety boundary, and falsifier;
- reader output is clear enough to use without opening the audit bundle; and
- every reader claim resolves through the audit projection to captured source material.

## Completion and notification

The coordinator finishes only after all predicates in `AGENTS.md` are satisfied, the
merged main commit and fresh-main CI are verified, and the final artifacts include:

- the reader-first world-model report;
- the complete audit projection and machine-verifiable bundle;
- the coordinator ledger and independent review;
- exact commands, commits, PRs, CI runs, effect spend, and residual limitations.

Do not send routine status updates. Notify the owner only when the outcome is complete,
or when a protected authority boundary has blocked all useful progress after three
distinct strategies. Because July 17, 2026 is Friday, any release or deployment step
waits until Saturday July 18, 2026 at the earliest; from then it is authorized and the
loop performs it without waiting for the owner, completing the receipt afterward.
