Northwind Digital Infrastructure — 2025 Sustainability Report

Publisher: Northwind Digital Infrastructure Inc. (fixture operator). Published: 2025-08-05.

In 2024, 100 percent of the electricity consumed across our global fleet was matched with purchases of renewable energy certificates and power purchase agreements on an annual basis.

Our operations in the Interior Plains region are therefore powered by renewable energy.

We have pledged to be water positive by 2030, replenishing more water than our operations consume, and in 2024 we funded replenishment projects totaling 2.1 million cubic meters.

Our two Interior Plains campuses support 1,400 direct and contractor jobs and contributed 14.2 million dollars in state and local taxes in 2024.

Backup generation at our campuses ran for fewer than 40 hours per site in 2024, exclusively for required testing and two grid-emergency events.
