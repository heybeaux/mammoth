2009 Report on Server Farm Electricity Use

Publisher: National Energy Analytics Office (fixture agency). Published: 2009-05-20. Superseded: yes, by later assessments.

Data centers consumed approximately 1.5 percent of national electricity in 2008.

Typical enterprise facilities operate at a power usage effectiveness near 2.0.

The report projects national data-center electricity demand will double by 2015 absent efficiency improvements.
