Study questions data center power claims

Publisher: Plains Business Wire (fixture syndication outlet). Published: 2026-03-01. Attribution: reprinted from Grid Integrity Project materials.

Annual renewable-energy-certificate matching does not demonstrate carbon-free operation on an hourly basis, according to a new report.

Using settlement data, the report finds that data-center campuses in the Interior Plains balancing area drew 62 percent of their overnight electricity from gas and coal units in 2025, despite operators' annual matching claims.

The group estimates that each additional gigawatt of inflexible data-center load extends coal-unit operation in the region by 14 months on average.
