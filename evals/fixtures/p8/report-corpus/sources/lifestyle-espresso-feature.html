The quiet rise of home espresso

Publisher: Sunday Lifestyle Magazine (fixture). Published: 2026-02-06.

Across the county, kitchen counters are making room for lever machines and single-dose grinders as home espresso becomes the weekend hobby of choice.

Local roasters report that light-roast single origins now outsell traditional blends, and tasting clubs are forming in community centers.

Enthusiasts say the appeal is data-driven precision at home: dialing in grind size, dose, and shot time until the cup tastes just right.

The trend shows no sign of slowing, with waiting lists for popular machines stretching into the summer.
