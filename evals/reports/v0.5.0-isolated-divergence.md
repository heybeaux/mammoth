## Scope and authority

P5 adds commit-before-reveal isolated divergence, sanitized blind-review context,
deterministic assignment/correlation policy, dissent and residue retention,
budget reservation/settlement/release accounting, cancellation receipts, live
Temporal execution/recovery gates, and read-only Observatory projection for the
P5 isolation surface.

Postgres/CAS remains authoritative for product state. Temporal remains an
orchestration control plane and replay/recovery evidence source, not a product
state store. Observatory projection remains read-only and fail-closed.

## Known limitations and P6 deferrals

- P5 proves isolated divergence and blind review gates; it does not claim broader
  landscape, prior-art, falsification, experiment, synthesis, or topology
  planning cells.
- Provider-dependent live model quality remains outside offline CI.
- Managed production deployment, managed Postgres, managed object storage, and
  managed Temporal operations remain outside this checkpoint.
- The 3D Observatory UI remains deferred. P5 supplies read-only projection and
  operator-inspection evidence for isolation, attribution, dissent, spend,
  cancellation, and recovery.
- Memory, Temporal history, model agreement, and reviewer consensus remain
  non-authoritative for truth.
