# M2 runtime composition receipt

## Outcome

The local runtime composes the checked-in RFC 2606 fixture through retrieval,
immutable raw and parsed snapshots, exact location, deterministic claim proposal,
named evidence-policy assessment, authoritative ledger persistence, and dossier
compilation.

The supported claim renders with a sentence trace to its exact locator and
snapshot digest. The HTTPS guarantee proposal remains unresolved, is persisted
and listed in the manifest, and cannot appear as supported prose.

## Recovery evidence

The runtime exposes stage fault hooks. Its recovery fixture interrupts after the
ledger commit, creates a fresh logical execution, and reaches the same completed
result without duplicating evidence, claims, or the provider-idempotent snapshot
effect receipt.

## Verification

```sh
pnpm verify:m2
```

This runs the independent fixture-integrity suite and the runtime composition,
provenance, fail-closed, durable-artifact, and restart tests.
