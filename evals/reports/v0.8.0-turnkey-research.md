## Limitations

- Search snippets are discovery hints only and are excluded from evidence.
- Live public-web coverage is bounded by configured budget and the acquired source
  set; the report preserves unresolved gaps instead of fabricating closure.
- The local CLI is the P8 product surface. Hosted API, desktop UI, full
  Observatory visualization, browser-rendered/OCR-heavy media extraction, and
  Parliament provider execution remain future work.
- The deterministic offline verifier remains fixture-backed by design; live
  provider quality, cost, and reliability are recorded only in the authorized
  live exhibition receipt.
